## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our threat feed and tools seriously.

If you discover a security vulnerability (e.g., sensitive data leakage in the feed, or a vulnerability in the Python SDK), please **DO NOT** open a public issue.

### How to report
Please email the maintainer directly at: **[yuexuan521@gmail.com]** .

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for the fix.
